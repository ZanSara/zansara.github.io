Real Life Subtitles

Have you ever struggled to listen to a native speaker of the language you’re learning and wished you could see subtitles in real life as well?

With Real Life Subtitles, the problem is solved ✨ Just open this page, insert your Deepgram API key, press the Listen button and enjoy your realtime transcriptions!

And if you want to experiment, you can fork it on GitHub and make your own.
